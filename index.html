// 01 — The Problem

Every existing system assumes
you have internet.
You don't.

Disaster zones, rural clinics, field missions — the places that need coordination the most are the places with no connectivity. Existing platforms fail the moment you need them most.

No connectivity in the field
Hurricanes destroy infrastructure. Remote villages never had it. Cloud-based tools go dark the moment disaster strikes.
Fragmented team awareness
Five responders working five devices with no shared picture. Duplicated work, missed patients, decisions made on incomplete data.
Paper in a digital world
Most field teams still rely on paper clipboards and Google Forms. Data disappears. Reporting takes days. No one learns anything.
Systems built for hospitals
Epic, Athena, even OpenMRS — designed for stable infrastructure, IT staff, and permanent clinics. Not for a tent in a disaster zone.
// 02 — Platform

Queue. Chart. Record.
Report. One system.

Offline-First Architecture
Every patient record, queue position, and data point lives on the device. No connectivity needed — ever — to operate at full capacity.
Conflict-Free Sync
Built on cr-sqlite CRDT technology. When devices reconnect — over local Wi-Fi, hotspot, or LTE — all records merge automatically. No duplicates, no conflicts, no manual cleanup.
Patient Queue & Triage
From the moment someone walks in, they're in the system. Queue management, triage priority, and patient flow — visible to every device on the team instantly.
Clinical Charting
Medical, dental, pharmacy, and specialty documentation in a single streamlined interface. Bilingual English/Spanish. Designed for non-technical operators.
AI Health Reporting
On-device AI generates community health reports from mission data — top diagnoses, treatment patterns, population trends. No cloud required. No data leaves the device.
Longitudinal Records
Patient history persists across deployments. Recurring organizations build a community health record over years — not just a snapshot from a single mission.

// Operational Timeline
Pre-Mission Configuration
Community data, staff accounts, and mission parameters loaded before departure. Tablets pre-configured — no setup required in the field.
Day 1: Full Clinic Operations
8 tablets operating simultaneously across registration, triage, medical, dental, and pharmacy. Every station sees the same patient in real time.
End-of-Day Sync
Local mesh sync over portable router. CRDT engine resolved all cross-device records automatically — no manual reconciliation.
Community Health Report
AI-generated mission report produced on-device — top diagnoses, treatment outcomes, population health insights for the foundation.
Cloud Sync on Return
Full encrypted dataset uploaded to analytics dashboard on return to connectivity. Longitudinal record updated for next deployment.
